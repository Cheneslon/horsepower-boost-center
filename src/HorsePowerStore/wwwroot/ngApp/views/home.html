What can we do for you? HorsePowerStore is dedicated to providing the best advice for upgrading your current vehicle to its maximum possible capacity, by suggesting safe, well trusted, and affordable products for purchase. Does this cost any money? None! just register by clicking the register button in the nav bar, and navigate to the Car Search page. Follow the onscreen instructions to view a list of matching products that will work best with your vehicle. Where does this information come from? HorsePowerStore is run on a blend of trusted commercial data from companies like Edmunds and Mecum, and homebrew data collected from real-life experienced mechanics who have spent their day to day working with the parts we advertise. What do i do with the parts i like? Add the parts of interest to you to your cart, and then view vender locations and prices in your cart on the "My Cars" page. I have multiple cars, do i need to fill this form out again? Yes, however you can save your car to your account, and organize carts of products by vehicle for ease of use.
